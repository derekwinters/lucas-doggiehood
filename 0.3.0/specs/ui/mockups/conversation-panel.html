Conversation panel

Opens when you tap a dog's speech bubble. The dog states its request; you Accept or decline. It reuses the shared DialogueBox shell and PillButtons (#173), sits bottom-center so the dog and neighborhood stay visible above it, and adds the decline action that #185 needs.

Regions

Decline behavior (the decision): "Not now" dismisses the panel without accepting — non-punishing (no timers/fail states, per economy.md), and the dog keeps its speech bubble so you can reopen it. Tapping outside the panel = the same as "Not now".
